ACTaM 2024-25

Wrapping up...

With this project, we feel we have exceeded our expectations. What started as theoretical exercise is now, at good title, a musical instrument. It has its limitations, but is relatively easy to program and it behaves predictably. Moreover, it allows decent results in terms of sounds and playability and, in the end, this is what really makes the difference. For us, this has been a two months journey that has taught us a lot. Very probably, we will not stop here. There are so many things we have thought about in terms of implementations - part of them will be listed below in our Wish List section - and they call for action... Anyhow, it's time for us to freeze our results and present them just as they are now.
